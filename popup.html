Nanolab Core Hour Calculator
instructions

Navigate to main "Reservations" page, then click button above to see available nanolab hours.

After you click "get existing" you can check how many hours will be available at a future date.

  1. Log into mercuryweb
  2. Navigate to main Reservations page. show me
  3. Click "get current core hours" button in extension window.
  4. (optional) After step 3, you may enter a future date and click "calculate" to check how many core hours will be available at that future time.
